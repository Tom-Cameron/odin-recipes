Fuck Me Chicken

Home

Fuck me chicken is a twist on marry me chicken. A creamy sauce, with strong unami flavours

Ingredients

  • Bone-in Chicken Thigs: 500g
  • Bacon: 1 or 2 long rashers
  • Onion: 1
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tsp
  • Cream: 200ml

Method

  1. Season chicken thighs liberally wiht salt and pepper
  2. Heat oil in medium oven safe pan over medium heat, Sear chicken to give a lovely brown covering on both sides
  3. after about 4 minutes, remove chicken from pan, add onions, bacon, mushrooms, and garlic and continue cooking over medium heat
  4. Once bacon and veges have gotten colour, de-glaze pan with white wine, being sure to scrape the frond off the bottom of the pan
  5. add tomato paste and chicken stock, once combine, add back in the chicken thighs
  6. place in oven for 20 minutes uncovered
  7. remove thighs again and stir in cream over low heat
  8. serve sauce and add thigh ontop, garnish with finely chopped parsley
