Shakshuka

Home

Shakshuka is a simple yet beautiful dish of Eggs baked in a hearty tomato sauce, perfect for a cozy winter morning served with generously buttered toast

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil
  • 1 250g tinned tomato
  • 1 red Bell pepper
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt & Pepper

Method

  1. Slice the onion and garlic, and add to a oven save pan over medium heat with a generous amount of oil
  2. slice the Bell pepper into thin strips; cutting first in half through the stem, then into fine strips
  3. Once onion is soft and fragrent add the bell peppers and continue cooking on medium
  4. add the Cumin and paprika, as well as the tomato paste, tinned tomatoes, and the stock
  5. let simmer for about 5 minutes
  6. turn the heat low, and make 4 holes in the sauce, crack and add the eggs gently
  7. cook in the oven on 180C for 7-9 minutes
  8. carefully remove from oven and serve with warm buttered toast
