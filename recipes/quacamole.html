Quacomole is a Mexican dip, Avocado, combined with the perfect complimentary ingredients that give it just enough zing

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Avocados
  • 1/2 of a red onion
  • 1/2 of a jalapeno
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • Salt & Pepper

Method

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the seed, and add to small to medium bowl
  2. Very finely dice the red onion and jalapeno, add to the bowl
  3. finely chop the coriander and add it to the bowl
  4. Cut the lime in half, and add the juice of both halves to the bowl
  5. mash and stir the ingredients together using a fork
  6. add salt, pepper, and more lime juice to taste
  7. Serve with Tortilla chips, or as topping for nachos or tacos
